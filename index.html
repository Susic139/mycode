Welcome to eCycle

Your trusted partner in managing electronic waste through responsible recycling and disposal methods. Join us in protecting the environment by recycling your old electronics.

What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste (e-waste) is discarded electronic or electrical devices such as phones, computers, and televisions. Improper disposal of these can cause environmental pollution and harm human health.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce the harmful effects of e-waste by providing efficient, environmentally-friendly recycling options and promoting awareness about e-waste management.
